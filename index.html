Temple history

This is our history from a long time ago

Wat lam chang has been long and long time ago. King Meng-rai of Chiangmai had built the temple for elephat structure . From A.D. 1292, while Chiang Mai city was under the constructions, King Meng-rai resided temporarily in Wat Chiang-Mun and invited King RamKhamHang of Sukhothai kingdom and King Ngam-Muang from Pra-yao Kingdom to given name of the city.At this time, they built elephant feeding area. named Wiang chiang chang. The elephants of tree kings was visited in this area, later the temple was built, named Wat Lam-Chang means Temple of Tetered of Elephant there for the elephant statue.
